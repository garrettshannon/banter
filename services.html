Services

Creative, Production & Post. Soup to Nuts.

Creative

Whether you're an agency bringing us an idea for a project or a brand hoping that we can bring one to you, we're here to bring that idea to life. In this phase, our team works with you every step of the way to ensure your goals are met. Project milestones, schedule and deliverables will be clearly defined from start to finish.

  • Strategy and scheduling
  • Treatments and scripting
  • Budget breakdown

Prep & Production

Pre-production is the "We'll take it from here" moment of our collaboration. We distill each aspect of the creative into a practical series of check boxes to ensure we have everything covered before we shoot. Throughout this process, we'll share things with you so you can approve specifics and know what's happening every step of the way. We'll determine where we shoot, how we're going to shoot it and who the actors are in front of the camera in order to create a cohesive spot that reflects the creative vision of the project.

  • Casting & locations
  • Art direction & design references
  • Shot lists & story boards

Production is the fun part! We'll work with our network of talented crew to make our project come to life. We'll use fun film jargon like "Crafty" and "C-47s" on set. It's not your job to know what that is, but you can trust that we do. Well, mostly. So strap yourselves in with us and watch the machine go go go, but we've also worked with clients who prefer a hands off approach and that's fine too.

  • Lights
  • Camera
  • Action

Post

Post Production is when we put it all together. We slice and dice our way through the footage to make the best possible version of our collaboration. Whether it's telling our interviewee's story, animating stylish graphics to accompany the actors, or simply adjusting the color of the sky this is where our collaboration will take it's shape. We'll share each cut with you and you'll tell us what you think, we'll make adjustments and wrap everything up in a nice little package. And that's that.

  • Editing, VFX & motion graphics
  • Color correction & sound mixing
  • Final deliverable exports & raw footage hand off
  • Hugs?
